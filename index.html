About Impact VTC

Impact Vocational Training Centre offers 100% scholarship programs to empower youth with practical skills like web development, graphic design, and video editing. Our mission is to create career-ready individuals through accessible, quality training.

Our Services

Web Development

Learn front-end and back-end development with hands-on projects using HTML, CSS, JS, and PHP.

Graphic Design

Master tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, and Canva to create professional designs for print and digital.

Video Editing

Learn video editing with Premiere Pro and CapCut to create impactful videos for social media and business.
